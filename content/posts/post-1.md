---
title: "On Building Things That Matter"
date: "March 15, 2024"
id: 1
---

There's something deeply satisfying about creating something from nothing. Whether it's code, art, or ideas, the process of building forces us to think clearly and solve problems creatively.

When I reflect on the projects that have brought me the most satisfaction, they all share a common thread: they solved a real problem for real people. It doesn't matter if it's a simple tool that saves someone 10 minutes a day or a complex system that powers a business—what matters is the impact.

The key is to start small and iterate. Perfect is the enemy of good, and good is often exactly what people need.
